About

Shready.net is a network dedicated to curating extreme sports history and planning future sessions. Starting with where and when people are skating, Shready will soon know How, Why people are skating. And all the information regarding past, present and future skating. The names of every trick and how to do them. Every awesome trick that has ever been done. This is only the beginning.
